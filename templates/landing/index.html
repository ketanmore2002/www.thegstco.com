What is a Virtual Office Address for GST Registration or VPOB?

In Simple Words, Virtual Address is a physical address that can be used for all business correspondence and mails. Today eCommerce Sellers need a Virtual Office Address to Store and Sell their Products in Multiple Fulfillment Centre's, This will help Amazon and Flipkart Sellers grow their Sales and Reach by Providing their customers with Fast Delivery.


What is Multi-State GST Number?

If a business operates in more than one state, a separate GST registration is required for each state.
For example, if a Mobile Shop sells in both Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh, he must apply for separate GST enrolment in each state.
In the Same way, eCommerce Sellers willing to store inventory in Delhi & Hyderabad FC need to obtain GST in each state.
